HACKATHON DETAIILS

Smart India Hackathon:

Organized by the Ministry of Education and All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), the Smart India Hackathon is one of the largest hackathons in the world. It brings together students from various colleges and universities to solve real-world challenges faced by government departments and industries. The hackathon focuses on innovation and encourages participants to develop technological solutions that can address pressing issues across different sectors.

Code Gladiators:

Code Gladiators is a coding competition organized by TechGig, one of India's leading technology platforms. It attracts thousands of participants, including professional coders, software developers, and programming enthusiasts. The competition covers a wide range of coding challenges and technologies, with participants competing in various categories such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Cloud Computing, Data Science, and more.

Microsoft Imagine Cup:

The Microsoft Imagine Cup is a global technology competition that provides a platform for students to showcase their innovative ideas and projects. The competition encourages participants to leverage Microsoft technologies and platforms to develop solutions that can have a positive impact on society. The Indian edition of Imagine Cup sees participation from talented students across the country, with finalists getting the opportunity to present their projects on a global stage.
