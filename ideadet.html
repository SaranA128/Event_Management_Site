IDEATHON DETAIILS

AngelHack:

AngelHack is a series of hackathons held worldwide, including several events in India. It focuses on fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and collaboration. Participants form teams and work on developing innovative solutions to specific challenges or problem statements. AngelHack events often attract a diverse range of participants, including developers, designers, and entrepreneurs, and provide opportunities to connect with mentors, investors, and industry experts.

IBM Call for Code:

IBM Call for Code is a global initiative that encourages developers and technologists to create solutions addressing pressing societal issues, such as natural disasters, climate change, and public health emergencies. The challenge invites participants to use IBM technologies and resources to develop scalable and sustainable applications that can make a difference in disaster response and recovery efforts. Indian participants actively contribute to this global tech challenge.

Google Code Jam:

Google Code Jam is a prestigious annual coding competition organized by Google. It invites programmers from around the world, including India, to showcase their coding skills and problem-solving abilities. Participants compete in multiple rounds of algorithmic challenges, with top performers advancing to subsequent stages. Google Code Jam offers an opportunity to engage with the global coding community and win prizes.
